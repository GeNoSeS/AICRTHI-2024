AICRTHI-2024

Annual International Conference on Recent Trends in Healthcare Innovation

May 24th and May 25th - 2024

@VVCE(Mysore,Karnataka,India)

The Annual International Conference on Recent Trends in Healthcare Innovation, often abbreviated as AICRTHI, stands as a pivotal event on the global healthcare calendar. This prestigious conference brings together healthcare professionals, researchers, innovators, and industry leaders from around the world to engage in meaningful discussions, share cutting-edge research, and explore the latest trends and innovations in the field of healthcare. AICRTHI serves as a dynamic platform where diverse perspectives converge, fostering collaboration, knowledge exchange, and the dissemination of ground-breaking ideas that have the potential to reshape the landscape of healthcare.



The aim of the " Annual International Conference on Recent Trends in Healthcare Innovation (AICRTHI)" is to foster collaboration , knowledge exchange, and innovation in the intersection of healthcare and advanced technologies. This event seeks to provide a forum where professionals from diverse backgrounds can come together to :

1.Showcase the latest advancements in AI and ML applications within the healthcare sector.
2.Discuss the challenges and opportunities in healthcare.
3.Promote ethical considerations in the development and deployment of engineering technologies in healthcare.
4.Facilitate interdisciplinary dialogue to accelerate healthcare innovation.
5.Encourage international cooperation in research, development, and implementation of different solutions for healthcare.

Call For Papers



The "International Conference on AI and ML in Healthcare" aspires to be a catalyst for positive change, where ideas flourish, partnerships form, and transformative solutions emerge, ultimately benefiting patients and healthcare systems worldwide. The conference aims to cover a wide range of topics related to machine learning and computing systems, including but not limited to:



    • AI and ML in Medical Imaging
    • Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS)
    • Electronic Health Records (EHR) and Data Integration
    • Natural Language Processing (NLP) in Healthcare
    • Telemedicine and Remote Patient Monitoring
    • Healthcare Robotics and Automation
    • Pharmaceutical Research and Drug Discovery
    • Patient-Centric Care and Personalized Medicine
    • Healthcare Analytics and Population Health Management
    • Case Studies and Real-World Applications
    • AI in Healthcare Operations and Management
